png2mp4

09/17/2016 de Snip

Convert a folder with enumerated .png files into a x264 Video i a .mp4 container with a given frame rate.

ffmpeg -f image2 -pattern_type glob -framerate 60 -i "img*.png" -vcodec libx264 vid.mp4

svg2png

09/17/2016 de Snip

Convert .svg into .png with white background.

inkscape -z -b \"#fff\" -e img.png -h 1080 img.svg

Or a complete folder:

for i in *.svg
    do inkscape -z -b \"#fff\" -e $(basename -s .svg $i).png -h 1080 $i
done
